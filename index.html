START BUILDING!

The barrier to building software is gone. You no longer need to be a hardcore software engineer typing endless lines of code to launch a tech company.

The Bradford Agentic Hackathon is an intensive innovation sprint. We are bringing together 100 of Yorkshire's most ambitious individuals to build functional, AI-driven Minimum Viable Products (MVPs) in a single day.

You will use natural language "vibe coding" and OpenClaw agentic orchestration to build real-world tech products that actually work. If you can write a prompt, you can build an app.

Who Should Apply?

This event is open to everyone.

The Tech Builders

Developers and AI enthusiasts ready to master agentic orchestration and build resilient backends.

Commercial Founders

Entrepreneurs, Business Strategists, and Marketers focusing on market viability, pitching, and solving real industry problems.

The Visionaries

UI/UX experts and non-technical innovators with bold creative ideas. If you can envision it, you can prompt it.

Don't know how to code? Good. Bring your ideas, and let the AI do the heavy lifting.
